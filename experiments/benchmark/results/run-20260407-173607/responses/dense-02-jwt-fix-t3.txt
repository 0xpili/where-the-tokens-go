In the meantime — "token expired" on a fresh token is almost always one of these causes:

1. **Clock skew** — issuer and validator clocks differ; validator sees `exp` in the past
2. **Milliseconds vs seconds** — `exp` set with `Date.now()` (ms) instead of `Math.floor(Date.now()/1000)` (s)
3. **`iat` used as `exp`** — accidentally comparing issue time against itself
4. **Wrong timezone** — clocks agree numerically but different TZ offsets
